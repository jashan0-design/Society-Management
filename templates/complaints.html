❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to resolve a complaint?

Complaints are typically resolved within 7-10 business days. Urgent issues like water/electricity outages are given priority and resolved within 24 hours.

How do I track my complaint status?

Use the "Track Status" section above. Enter your SOC-XXXXXX ID to get real-time updates on your complaint.

Can I withdraw a complaint?

Yes, complaints can be withdrawn within 24 hours of filing. After that, please contact the office directly.

Who should I contact for emergency issues?

For emergencies, contact the office at +91-9876543210 or visit the office during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM).
